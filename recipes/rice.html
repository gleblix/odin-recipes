Rice

Description

Rice is the backbone of many regional cuisines. Having the skill to quickly and reliably make a tasty pot of rice is essential for the proficient cook. This recipe will show you how to make rice that is fluffy, delicious, and consistent.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Rice
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice 3-4 times in room temperature water to remove excess starch. This will keep the rice from having a gooey texture after cooking.
  2. Put the rice in a cooking pot with double the volume of water.
  3. Add a tablespoon of butter and ground pepper to the water.
  4. Set the pot to high heat. As the butter dissolves, mix the water solution and the rice to evenly distribute the butter and pepper.
  5. As soon as the water begins boiling, place a lid on the pot and reduce the heat to low/simmer. Cook for 20 min.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
