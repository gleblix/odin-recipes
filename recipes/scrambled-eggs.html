Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are a basic breakfast staple, and yet many people struggle to pull off this core dish. Prove your basic culinary chops and impress friends and family!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt an ample amount of butter into a preferably nonstick pan, at least 1tbsp for a small pan and more as needed. Heat the pan to medium-medium low heat.
  2. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl and whisk with a fork until they reach a uniform consistency.
  3. Pour the eggs into the heated pan. Cook the eggs 'low and slow', stirring consistently to avoid forming a cooked layer of eggs on the bottom of the pan.
  4. As you keep stirring, continue to mix the uncooked eggs into the cooked parts, keeping the mixture moist and soft.
  5. Take off heat and serve before the eggs dry out.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
