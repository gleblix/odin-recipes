Buttered Noodles

Description

Buttered noodles are simple to make and delicious to eat! With just a few simple ingredients you can produce this simple but always reliably delightful dish for yourself, and it is sure to please any guests who may stop by!

With just a bit of practice, you can master this straightforward dish and add it to your culinary toolbox.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a roiling boil.
  3. Stir in fettuccine, bring back to a boil, and cook pasta over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Drain and return pasta to pot. Mix butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
